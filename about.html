    I am a computational and structural biologist focused on sequence → structure → function modeling, protein/peptide design, small molecular drug screening and translational multi-omics. I develop end-to-end AI pipelines that turn genome/epigenome/transcriptome data into mechanistic insight, biomarkers, and actionable targets—especially for neurodevelopmental and viral disease.

    Recent work centers on precision diagnostics in pediatric cerebral palsy, variant effect prediction, and target/lead discovery across viral and oncology pathways. I also mentor and build community as founder of BioSol Centre (Bangladesh).

    Focus

    • Variant interpretation

    • Protein & peptide design

    • Translational multi-omics
